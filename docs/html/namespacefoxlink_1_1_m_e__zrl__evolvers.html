Calculate all time derivatives necessary to solve the moment expansion evolution of the Fokker-Planck equation of zero rest length (zrl) crosslinkers bound to moving rods.

d is the time derivative of corresponding variable

Parameters
r1Center of mass postion of rod1
r2Center of mass position of rod2
u1Orientation unit vector of rod1
u2Orientation unit vector of rod2
rhoZeroth motor moment
P1First motor moment of s1
P2First motor moment of s2
mu11Second motor moment of s1 and s2
mu20Second motor moment of s1
mu02Second motor moment of s2
gpara1Parallel drag coefficient of rod1
gperp1Perpendicular drag coefficient of rod1
grot1Rotational drag coefficient of rod1
gpara2Parallel drag coefficient of rod1
gperp2Perpendicular drag coefficient of rod1
grot2Rotational drag coefficient of rod1
voVelocity of motor when no force is applied
fsStall force of motor ends
koTurnover rate of motors
cEffective concentration of motors in solution
ksMotor spring constant
beta1/(Boltzmann's constant * Temperature)
L1Length of rod1
L2Length of rod2
fastFlag on whether or not to use fast solving techniques
Returns
: Time-derivatives of all time varying quantities in a flattened array
